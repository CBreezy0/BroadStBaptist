Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear to church?

We believe in "come as you are" worship! While some prefer to dress up for Sunday service, you'll see everything from casual to formal attire. The most important thing is that you're comfortable and ready to worship.

Is childcare available?

Currently, we do not offer childcare services during our worship services. Families are welcome to bring children of all ages to worship with us, and we encourage parents to help their children participate in our services as they are able.

Where should I park?

We have convenient parking available around our church building at 121 Morris Avenue. Our ushers and greeters are happy to direct you to available parking spaces.

What is your worship style?

Our worship combines traditional Baptist elements with contemporary touches. You'll experience heartfelt prayer, congregational singing of both hymns and contemporary songs, expository preaching from God's Word (KJV), and genuine fellowship.

How can I get more involved?

Start by filling out a connection card and speaking with one of our pastors or ministry leaders. We'll help you find the right ministry fit based on your interests, gifts, and availability.
