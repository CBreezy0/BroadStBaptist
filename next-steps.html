Your Journey with the Broad Street Family

Welcome to Your Journey at Broad Street

We're excited to help you take the next steps in your faith journey and become fully integrated into the Broad Street family. Whether you're visiting for the first time or ready to make this your church home, we've created a simple pathway to help you connect, grow, and serve.

Step 1: Attend a Service

The best way to experience who we are is to join us for worship! We invite you to attend any of our Sunday or Wednesday gatherings to experience authentic worship, biblical teaching, and genuine community.

Sunday School

9:30 AM - Bible study for all ages

Sunday Worship

11:00 AM - Our main worship service

Wednesday Bible Study

7:00 PM - Midweek spiritual growth

What to Expect: Come as you are! You'll find a warm welcome, inspiring worship, and practical biblical teaching that applies to your daily life.

Step 2: Connect with Us

We'd love to get to know you! Please fill out a connection card located in the pews or at the welcome center. This helps us provide you with personalized information and support.

Join a Connect Group: These small groups are a great way to meet people and grow in your faith. Groups meet throughout the week in various locations.

Step 3: Discover Our Church

Learn more about Broad Street's mission, values, and beliefs. We offer a Discover Class every month for newcomers to explore what it means to be a part of our church family.

Step 4: Get Involved

There are many ways to get involved at Broad Street! Consider joining a ministry team, participating in church events, or volunteering for community outreach.

Step 5: Consider Membership

Membership at Broad Street means committing to grow in your faith and serve alongside other believers. We invite you to attend our Next Steps Class to learn more about church membership.

Step 6: Grow and Serve

As you grow in your faith, we encourage you to find ways to serve others. This could be through ministry opportunities within the church or outreach programs in the community.
