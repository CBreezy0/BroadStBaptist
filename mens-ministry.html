Men's Ministry

The Men's Ministry exists to equip men of all ages to walk boldly in their God-given roles as husbands, fathers, friends, and leaders. We gather for Bible study, prayer, and accountability, plus service projects that put faith into action. Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking to deepen your walk with Christ, you'll find camaraderie, encouragement, and real-life discipleship here.

"Iron sharpeneth iron; so a man sharpeneth the countenance of his friend."

Proverbs 27:17 KJV

What We Offer

  • Bible Study: Deep dive into God's Word with practical application
  • Prayer Groups: Consistent prayer support and intercession
  • Accountability: Safe space for honest conversation and growth
  • Service Projects: Hands-on opportunities to serve our community
  • Fellowship: Building genuine friendships and brotherhood
  • Leadership Development: Growing in our roles as spiritual leaders

Our Mission

We believe men need other men to walk alongside them in their faith journey. Our ministry provides a place where men can be authentic, grow spiritually, and support one another in becoming the men God has called them to be.

Join Us

All men are welcome, regardless of where they are in their faith journey. Whether you're a new believer or have been walking with Christ for years, there's a place for you in our brotherhood. Visit our main Ministries page to get connected.
