Prayer Ministry

We believe prayer is the foundation of our faith and community. Join us in lifting up requests, praises, and intercession.

The Power of Prayer

At Broad Street Baptist Church, we believe that prayer is not just a religious duty, but a powerful privilege that connects us directly to our Heavenly Father. Through prayer, we find strength, guidance, comfort, and the miraculous touch of God in our daily lives.

"And this is the confidence that we have in him, that, if we ask any thing according to his will, he heareth us: And if we know that he hear us, whatsoever we ask, we know that we have the petitions that we desired of him."

1 John 5:14-15 KJV
