Missions & Outreach Ministry

God calls us to "go and make disciples of all nations," and our Missions & Outreach Ministry answers that call locally and globally. We partner with churches and relief organizations overseas, support a network of urban ministries in our city, and organize service projects that meet practical needs in our community. Whether you feel led to give, go, or pray, there's a place for you in our mission.

"Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost."

Matthew 28:19 KJV

Local Outreach

  • Community Service: Regular projects meeting practical needs in Birmingham
  • Food Distribution: Supporting local food banks and meal programs
  • Urban Ministries: Partnering with city organizations for maximum impact
  • Homeless Outreach: Providing meals, clothing, and hope to those in need
  • School Partnerships: Supporting local schools and students
  • Senior Care: Serving elderly residents in our community

Global Missions

  • Mission Partnerships: Supporting churches and missionaries worldwide
  • Relief Organizations: Contributing to disaster relief and humanitarian aid
  • Mission Trips: Opportunities for hands-on service abroad
  • Prayer Support: Consistent intercession for global ministry

Ways to Get Involved

There are many ways to participate in our mission work:

  • Give: Financial support for local and global missions
  • Go: Join service projects and mission trips
  • Pray: Intercede for our mission partners and those we serve
  • Organize: Help plan and coordinate outreach events

Join the Mission

Christ's call to make disciples is for every believer. Find your place in our mission work by connecting with us through our Ministries page. Together, we can make a difference in Birmingham and around the world.
