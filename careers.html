Welcome & Mission Focus

Welcome to Broad Street Missionary Baptist Church! We believe that every member of our church family has unique gifts and talents that God has given for the purpose of serving others. Volunteers play a vital role in helping us fulfill our mission to love God and serve our community with the heart of Christ.

"I beseech you therefore, brethren, by the mercies of God, that ye present your bodies a living sacrifice, holy, acceptable unto God, which is your reasonable service."

Our Commitment to Inclusivity

At Broad Street Missionary Baptist Church, we affirm that all ages and abilities are welcome to serve. We believe that every person has been blessed with unique gifts from God, and we are committed to gifts-based placement that honors your talents while serving our community. In Christ, we are one body with many parts, and every part is essential to our mission of love and service.
