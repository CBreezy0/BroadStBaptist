What We Offer

  • Dynamic Worship: Live music, spoken word, and engaging messages
  • Small Groups: Intimate discussions addressing real teenage challenges
  • Retreats: Annual getaways for deeper spiritual growth and community building
  • Mission Trips: Opportunities to serve and make a difference locally and beyond
  • Service Days: Regular projects that put faith into action
  • Real Talk: Honest conversations about faith, life, and growing up

Weekly Programs

Join us every Wednesday at 6:30 PM for our mid-week service, featuring live music, a relevant message, and small group discussions. Sundays at 9:00 AM, we gather for a time of worship and teaching specifically designed for middle and high school students.

Special Events & Activities

Throughout the year, we organize various events such as lock-ins, concerts, and community service projects. Our annual summer camp is a highlight, offering students a chance to grow in their faith and build lasting friendships.

Leadership Team

Our student ministry is led by a dedicated team of volunteers and staff who are passionate about helping students grow in their faith. For more information about our leaders, visit the Leadership page.

How to Get Involved

Getting involved is easy! Attend one of our weekly meetings, sign up for an event, or reach out to our leadership team. We also encourage parents to get involved through our Parent Ministry.
