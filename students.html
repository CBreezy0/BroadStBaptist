Student Ministry (Middle & High School)

Students in grades 6–12 are invited to plug into a vibrant community built on faith, fun, and real talk. Our worship services mix live music, spoken word, and small-group discussions addressing the challenges teenagers face today. Throughout the year we host retreats, mission trips, and service days that help students grow closer to Christ and one another.

"Let no man despise thy youth; but be thou an example of the believers, in word, in conversation, in charity, in spirit, in faith, in purity."

1 Timothy 4:12 KJV

What We Offer

  • Dynamic Worship: Live music, spoken word, and engaging messages
  • Small Groups: Intimate discussions addressing real teenage challenges
  • Retreats: Annual getaways for deeper spiritual growth and community building
  • Mission Trips: Opportunities to serve and make a difference locally and beyond
  • Service Days: Regular projects that put faith into action
  • Real Talk: Honest conversations about faith, life, and growing up

Building Community

Our student ministry isn't just about programs—it's about building genuine relationships where students can grow closer to Christ and one another. We create a safe space where questions are welcome, friendships are formed, and faith becomes real.

Join Us

Whether you're new to faith or have been walking with Christ for years, there's a place for you in our student community. Connect with us through our main Ministries page or come visit us during one of our regular gatherings.
