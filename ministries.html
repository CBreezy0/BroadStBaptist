Our Ministry Areas

At Broad Street Missionary Baptist Church, we believe every person has a purpose and place to serve God. Our ministries provide opportunities for spiritual growth, community connection, and meaningful service across all ages and life stages.

Children's Ministry

Welcome to our Children's Ministry, where we believe the next generation of Christ-followers matters deeply. Our Bible-based lessons help kids K–5 discover God's love through stories, songs, and crafts. Our dedicated volunteers ensure a safe, nurturing environment so parents can worship with peace of mind.

"Suffer little children, and forbid them not, to come unto me: for of such is the kingdom of God."

Matthew 19:14 KJV

Student Ministry (Middle & High School)

Students in grades 6–12 are invited to plug into a vibrant community built on faith, fun, and real talk. Our worship services mix live music, spoken word, and small-group discussions addressing the challenges teenagers face today. Throughout the year we host retreats, mission trips, and service days that help students grow closer to Christ and one another.

"Let no man despise thy youth; but be thou an example of the believers, in word, in conversation, in charity, in spirit, in faith, in purity."

1 Timothy 4:12 KJV
Learn More

Men's Ministry

The Men's Ministry exists to equip men of all ages to walk boldly in their God-given roles as husbands, fathers, friends, and leaders. We gather for Bible study, prayer, and accountability, plus service projects that put faith into action. Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking to deepen your walk with Christ, you'll find camaraderie, encouragement, and real-life discipleship here.

"Iron sharpeneth iron; so a man sharpeneth the countenance of his friend."

Proverbs 27:17 KJV

Women's Ministry

Our Women's Ministry cultivates a sisterhood of faith, offering Bible studies, retreats, and fellowship events tailored to every stage of life. Through small groups, mentoring relationships, and community outreach, we encourage women to grow spiritually, support one another, and shine Christ's light in our city.

"Strength and honour are her clothing; and she shall rejoice in time to come."

Proverbs 31:25 KJV

Worship Ministry

The Worship Ministry leads our congregation in heartfelt praise and adoration of Jesus Christ. From contemporary band sets to traditional hymns, our team of vocalists, instrumentalists, and tech volunteers strives to create an atmosphere where worship becomes a genuine encounter with God. Interested in using your musical gifts? We hold auditions quarterly and offer regular training workshops in sound, lighting, and stage presence.

"O come, let us sing unto the LORD: let us make a joyful noise to the rock of our salvation."

Psalm 95:1 KJV
Learn More

Care Ministry

The Care Ministry extends Christ's compassion to those in need—both inside our church family and beyond. We visit the sick, provide meals for those in transition or crisis, and offer prayer teams before and after each weekend service. If you're facing a hardship or would like to serve on a care team, we'd love to connect you with the support you need or the hands to help carry others' burdens.

"Bear ye one another's burdens, and so fulfil the law of Christ."

Galatians 6:2 KJV

Media & Technology Ministry

Behind every service live-streamed or bulletin printed is the Media & Technology Ministry at work. From operating cameras to designing graphics for social media and our website, this team uses available tools to amplify the gospel message. No tech background? No problem—new volunteers receive hands-on training in all areas: video, audio, and digital design.

Missions & Outreach Ministry

God calls us to "go and make disciples of all nations," and our Missions & Outreach Ministry answers that call locally and globally. We partner with churches and relief organizations overseas, support a network of urban ministries in our city, and organize service projects that meet practical needs in our community. Whether you feel led to give, go, or pray, there's a place for you in our mission.

"Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost."
