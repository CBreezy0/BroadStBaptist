Who We Are

Welcome to Broad Street Missionary Baptist Church

Located at 121 Morris Avenue in Birmingham, Alabama, Broad Street Missionary Baptist Church is a vibrant community of believers committed to following Jesus Christ and serving our neighbors. We are a family-oriented church where people of all ages and backgrounds come together to worship, learn, and grow in faith.

Our Mission

We exist to glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ through worship, fellowship, discipleship, and mission. Our heart is to see lives transformed by the gospel and to be a beacon of hope in our community.

What We Believe

As a Baptist church, we hold to the fundamental truths of Christianity found in the Bible. We believe in salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ, the importance of baptism by immersion, and the authority of Scripture in all matters of faith and practice.

Our Community

Whether you're new to faith, returning to church, or looking for a church home, you'll find a warm welcome at Broad Street. We believe in "come as you are" worship and strive to create an environment where everyone can encounter God's love and grace.

Join Us

We'd love to meet you! Join us for worship on Sundays or connect with us throughout the week.
