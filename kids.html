Welcome to Broad Street Kids

Welcome to our Children's Ministry, where we believe the next generation of Christ-followers matters deeply. Our Bible-based lessons help kids K–5 discover God's love through stories, songs, and crafts. Our dedicated volunteers ensure a safe, nurturing environment so parents can worship with peace of mind.

"Suffer little children, and forbid them not, to come unto me: for of such is the kingdom of God."

Matthew 19:14 KJV

What to Expect

  • Age-Appropriate Learning: Bible-based lessons designed specifically for kids K–5
  • Interactive Activities: Stories, songs, crafts, and games that make learning fun
  • Safe Environment: Dedicated volunteers ensure a nurturing, secure space
  • Character Building: Teaching values and principles that last a lifetime

When We Meet

Children's Ministry meets during our regular worship services, providing age-appropriate programming while parents participate in the main service.

Get Your Child Involved

We'd love to welcome your child into our ministry! For more information about our Children's Ministry or to get your child registered, please contact us or visit our main Ministries page to fill out our involvement form.
