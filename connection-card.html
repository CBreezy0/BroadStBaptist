What Happens Next?

After you submit your connection card, here's what you can expect:

  • Personal Follow-Up: One of our pastors or ministry leaders will reach out based on your preferences
  • Welcome Package: If you're new, we'll send you information about our church and upcoming events
  • Ministry Connection: We'll help connect you with the ministries and groups you expressed interest in
  • Prayer Support: Your prayer requests will be shared with our prayer team (with your permission)
