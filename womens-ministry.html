Women's Ministry

Our Women's Ministry cultivates a sisterhood of faith, offering Bible studies, retreats, and fellowship events tailored to every stage of life. Through small groups, mentoring relationships, and community outreach, we encourage women to grow spiritually, support one another, and shine Christ's light in our city.

"Strength and honour are her clothing; and she shall rejoice in time to come."

Proverbs 31:25 KJV

What We Offer

  • Bible Studies: In-depth study of God's Word in small group settings
  • Retreats: Annual getaways for spiritual renewal and fellowship
  • Fellowship Events: Regular gatherings for connection and community
  • Small Groups: Intimate groups for deeper relationships and growth
  • Mentoring: Connecting women across generations for mutual support
  • Community Outreach: Serving together to impact our city for Christ

Bible Studies & Small Groups

Our Bible studies are designed to deepen your understanding of Scripture and foster meaningful connections with other women. We offer various studies throughout the year, focusing on different books of the Bible, topical studies, and seasonal studies such as Advent and Lent.

Small groups provide an intimate setting for discussion, prayer, and support. These groups meet regularly and are a great way to build lasting friendships and grow in your faith.

Retreats & Special Events

We host annual retreats that offer a time of spiritual renewal, teaching, and fellowship. These retreats are held in peaceful, inspiring locations and include time for worship, reflection, and fun activities.

Special events such as conferences, workshops, and social gatherings are held throughout the year. These events feature guest speakers, engaging activities, and opportunities to connect with other women in the church.

Mentoring & Life Stages

Our mentoring program pairs younger women with more mature women in the faith for guidance, support, and encouragement. This intergenerational ministry helps women navigate different life stages and grow in their relationship with Christ.

We also offer resources and training for women who wish to become mentors, equipping them to share their wisdom and experiences with others.

Community Outreach

We believe in being the hands and feet of Jesus in our community. Our outreach projects include serving at local shelters, food banks, and other community organizations. We also organize donation drives, awareness campaigns, and volunteer opportunities for women to get involved and make a difference.

Through these activities, we aim to share the love of Christ and meet the practical needs of those around us.

Join Our Sisterhood

We welcome all women to join our ministry, regardless of age, background, or church membership. To get involved, please visit our Ministries page for information on upcoming events, Bible studies, and small groups.

You can also contact us directly for more information on how to participate, volunteer, or become a mentor. We look forward to welcoming you into our sisterhood!
