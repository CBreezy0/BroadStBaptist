Women's Ministry

Our Women's Ministry cultivates a sisterhood of faith, offering Bible studies, retreats, and fellowship events tailored to every stage of life. Through small groups, mentoring relationships, and community outreach, we encourage women to grow spiritually, support one another, and shine Christ's light in our city.

"Strength and honour are her clothing; and she shall rejoice in time to come."

Proverbs 31:25 KJV

What We Offer

  • Bible Studies: In-depth study of God's Word in small group settings
  • Retreats: Annual getaways for spiritual renewal and fellowship
  • Fellowship Events: Regular gatherings for connection and community
  • Small Groups: Intimate groups for deeper relationships and growth
  • Mentoring: Connecting women across generations for mutual support
  • Community Outreach: Serving together to impact our city for Christ

For Every Stage of Life

Whether you're a young mother, career woman, empty nester, or grandmother, our ministry offers opportunities for growth and connection. We believe every woman has something to offer and something to learn from others in our community.

Join Our Sisterhood

Come as you are and discover the joy of Christian sisterhood. We'd love to welcome you into our community of faith. Connect with us through our Ministries page to learn about upcoming events and small groups.
