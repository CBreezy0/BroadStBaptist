How We Serve

  • Hospital & Home Visits: Providing comfort and prayer for the sick and shut-in
  • Meal Ministry: Coordinating meals for families in transition or crisis
  • Prayer Teams: Available before and after services for prayer support
  • Grief Support: Walking alongside families during times of loss
  • Emergency Assistance: Helping with immediate needs in our community
  • Encouragement Cards: Sending notes of hope and support

Need Care?

If you or someone you know is facing illness, loss, or other hardships, please don't hesitate to reach out. Our care team is here to provide practical support, emotional encouragement, and spiritual comfort during difficult times.

Join Our Care Team

We're always looking for compassionate individuals to join our care ministry. Whether you can visit, cook, pray, or simply offer a listening ear, there's a place for you to serve. Contact us through our Ministries page to get involved.
