Worship Ministry

The Worship Ministry leads our congregation in heartfelt praise and adoration of Jesus Christ. From contemporary band sets to traditional hymns, our team of vocalists, instrumentalists, and tech volunteers strives to create an atmosphere where worship becomes a genuine encounter with God. Interested in using your musical gifts? We hold auditions quarterly and offer regular training workshops in sound, lighting, and stage presence.

"O come, let us sing unto the LORD: let us make a joyful noise to the rock of our salvation."

Psalm 95:1 KJV

Our Worship Style

  • Contemporary Band Sets: Modern worship songs with full band accompaniment
  • Traditional Hymns: Classic songs of faith that have inspired generations
  • Blended Services: A mix of old and new that speaks to every heart
  • Special Music: Solos, duets, and choir performances throughout the year

Musical Teams & Opportunities

We're always looking for passionate individuals to join our worship team:

  • Vocalists: Lead singers and background vocalists
  • Instrumentalists: Piano, guitar, bass, drums, and more
  • Tech Volunteers: Sound engineering, lighting, and stage management
  • Support Team: Setup, breakdown, and equipment maintenance

Technical Ministry

Join our technical team to help create the worship environment:

  • Sound Engineers: Operate and maintain sound equipment
  • Lighting Technicians: Design and manage stage lighting
  • Media Operators: Control video and presentation software
  • Stage Managers: Oversee stage setup and transitions

Auditions & Training

We believe in developing each team member's gifts. We offer:

  • Quarterly auditions for new team members
  • Regular training workshops in sound, lighting, and stage presence
  • Mentorship from experienced team members
  • Opportunities for growth and leadership development

Get Involved

Ready to use your musical gifts in worship? Contact us through our Ministries page and let us know about your interests and experience. We'd love to have you join our team in creating an atmosphere of genuine worship and encounter with God.
